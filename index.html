    Biography

    I was born in Beirut, Lebanon, in 1983. I received my Master's degree in mathematics from Université d'Aix-Marseille in 2008 and the Ph.D. degree from Université de Montpellier in 2011. After postdoctoral positions in several labs (L2S, IMJ-PRG, QUARTZ), I got in 2017 a permanent position of Associate Professor at ENSEA within QUARTZ laboratory.
